About Our Hotel

Tehran hotel is located in the heart of the city, offering comfortable rooms and excellent service. The four-star hotel in Tehran, located on karimkhan street, was opened in 1380 and was renovated in June 1393. Other features of the hotel include luxury Iranian, French and Chinese restaurants with a warm and intimate environment with a combination of art and beauty, with traditional live music and the most diverse Iranian, European and East Asian cuisines, memorable moments and It will leave beautiful memories in the minds of dear guests!
